    • Types of Windows We Install in Paramus, NJ

    Our vast selection of window types allows homeowners and business owners to choose the perfect solution to meet their needs and style preferences.

    1. Double Hung Windows

    Double hung windows are a classic choice that features two operable sashes that slide vertically. These are ideal for home windows and office windows because of their easy cleaning and excellent ventilation.

    2. Bow Windows

    Bow windows consist of four or more windows that create a curved projection. These are perfect for adding charm and extra light to your house windows in living rooms or bedrooms.

    3. Bay Windows

    Bay windows are similar to bow windows but consist of three windows forming an angular projection. Bay windows enhance both indoor space and natural light.

    4. Vinyl Windows

    Vinyl windows are cost-effective, low-maintenance, and energy-efficient. They are ideal for both home windows and commercial windows.

    5. Aluminum Windows

    Known for their strength and slim profiles, aluminum windows are perfect for office windows and other commercial settings.
